# Product Overview

## Product Name
*(Not yet named — working title: Super App)*

## What It Is
A mobile-first super app platform inspired by WeChat, combining real-time messaging, a social feed, peer-to-peer payments, and an embedded mini-program ecosystem into one unified experience. The goal: users never need to switch between multiple apps for communication, social sharing, or everyday transactions.

## Problem
People depend on 3–5 separate apps daily just to message friends, share moments, and pay for things. This fragmentation creates friction: split notifications, disconnected wallets, no unified identity across platforms. There is no single trusted platform that handles communication, social, and financial interactions seamlessly in one place.

## Solution
A super app that starts with best-in-class messaging and expands outward — real-time chats, social moments, voice/video calls, peer-to-peer payments, and a mini-program ecosystem — so users do everything in one place. Developers can reach users without separate installs; merchants can transact within conversations.

## Target Users
- **Primary — Daily Communicators**: Mobile-first users aged 18–45 who rely heavily on messaging and social sharing every day.
- **Secondary — Small Businesses & Merchants**: Businesses wanting to reach and transact with customers through an integrated in-app channel.
- **Tertiary — Developers**: Builders creating lightweight mini-programs distributed to users inside the platform ecosystem.

## Core Features (MVP — Messaging Foundation First)
1. **1:1 Messaging** — Real-time text, image, voice note, and emoji exchange between users.
2. **Group Chats** — Multi-participant chat rooms with admin controls and file sharing.
3. **Moments / Social Feed** — Status updates, photos, and short posts; friends react and comment.
4. **Voice & Video Calls** — In-app calling with no need to leave the platform.
5. **Contacts & Discovery** — Add friends via QR code, phone number, or username search.
6. **Digital Wallet & Payments** — Send and receive money peer-to-peer; pay merchants directly in-app.
7. **Mini Programs** — Lightweight embedded apps (food ordering, games, local services) hosted within the platform.

## Positioning
The unified super app — one platform for messaging, social connection, and everyday transactions. Built mobile-first for daily active use at scale.

## Brand & Tone
Modern, trustworthy, and approachable. The platform feels like a daily companion, not a utility — clean UI, fast interactions, minimal onboarding friction. Warm but efficient.

## Strategic Principles
- **Messaging first**: Build messaging excellence as the gravitational core; expand to payments and mini-programs as the user network grows.
- **Low friction onboarding**: Phone number or social login → first message in under 60 seconds.
- **Privacy & security as differentiators**: End-to-end encryption and transparent data practices.
- **Developer ecosystem as moat**: Mini-program platform creates a long-term distribution advantage.
- **Monetization via transactions**: Platform fee on payments and mini-program commerce — not ads.

## Scope (Phase 1 MVP)
Initial build focuses on the messaging core: 1:1 chat, group chat, contacts, and basic user profiles. The broader super-app surface — digital wallet, social moments, voice/video, and mini-programs — rolls out in subsequent phases as the user network matures.
